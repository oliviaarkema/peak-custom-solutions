Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 2026

Our Commitment

Peak Custom Solutions is committed to making this website accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We strive to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

Features

  • All images include descriptive alt text
  • Pages use semantic HTML heading structure
  • Color contrast meets WCAG AA minimums
  • Navigation is keyboard-accessible
  • Forms include labeled inputs

Known Limitations

Some third-party content — including Machinery Pete and Facebook Marketplace listings linked from this site — may not meet accessibility standards. We have no control over third-party platforms.

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier on this site, please contact us. We take feedback seriously and will make reasonable efforts to address issues promptly.

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing effort. We review and improve our site regularly as standards and technology evolve.
