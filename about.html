After years of experience in machinery and agricultural equipment sales, procurement, and consulting, Rob Van Harten launched Peak Custom Solutions to provide customers with knowledgeable, relationship-driven service across multiple industries.

With extensive expertise in equipment sourcing, transportation, powersports, automotive, and specialty markets, Peak Custom Solutions helps customers navigate equipment decisions with confidence and trusted guidance.

The company was built on a commitment to integrity, industry knowledge, and delivering dependable solutions tailored to each customer’s needs.
